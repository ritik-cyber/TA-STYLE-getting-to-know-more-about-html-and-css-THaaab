This is my first article

Cat is a very adorable and a cute animal. It is a domestic animal and is kept as a
pet. It has very sharp claws and keen eyes that help it in seeing during the night.
That means that it has a very good nocturnal vision that is much better than humans.
It has two small ears, with a highly sensitive tympanic membrane (eardrum), which
helps it in hearing even the slightest of the sounds. It’s small and bushy tail helps it
in maintaining balance while walking.
